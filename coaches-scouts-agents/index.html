European Coaches

Coaches may attend teaching sessions with Rick Mahorn by advance arrangement.

The attendance fee is 25 € for one day, and 40 € for two days. Coaches wishing to observe on a more extended basis and coaches sending a player to the camp are invited to contact us to make other arrangements. Daily camp sessions will be closed, and only those coaches who have arranged to attend or sign up at the door will be admitted.

European Scouts, Team Representatives and Agents

Team representatives, coaches and agents are welcome to attend the two games on July 9 and July 12 (Belgrade Camp) and July 20 (Riga Camp).

For exact times and to confirm your participation, please contact the camp administration at  RickMahornBigManCamp@gmail.com

ANY COACH, AGENT, SCOUT, TEAM REPRESENTATIVE IS REQUESTED TO CONTACT THE CAMP ADMINISTRATORS IN ADVANCE AT RickMahornBigManCamp@gmail.com
