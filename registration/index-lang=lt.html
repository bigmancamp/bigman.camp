Camp Registration Information

CAMP REGISTRATION FEES

The camp fee covers your participation in the camp, but DOES NOT INCLUDE health insurance in the event of injury. Participants are highly encouraged to come with their own health insurance coverage.

Please note the reduced rate for those making payment by the early deadline, as indicated below:

Partial scholarships are available to those unable to afford the quoted prices but you MUST contact us at bigman.mentalcoaching@gmail.com, and priority in assistance will be given to those who register by 12.6 (Belgrade) or 19.6 (Riga).

Please note also that the number of participants is limited to help ensure a quality learning experience, and your spot is only guaranteed once payment has been received. Participants may make payment on the first day of camp, should space still be available.
