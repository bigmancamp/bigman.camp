FEES

 

Given the unique nature and high quality of our camp, the fees for the regular camp are LESS than what it costs us per player, however we are looking to find sponsors so that we can offer you an even further reduced fee. (Read below for information on scholarships to bring the cost even lower)

 

Regular Camp:     1199 €   ($1,350)Elite Players Camp     1,999 €   ($2,250)

 

The Regular Camp fee includes

  • Hotel and meals from dinner on May 31 through breakfast on June 10
  • Camp t-shirt and 2 game uniforms
  • All equipment, use of weight room

 

The Elite Players Camp fee (for selected players requesting this camp) includes

  • Single hotel room and all meals as above
  • Option to take part in scheduled games as noted on schedule
  • Additional small group and individual work out sessions with Rick
  • Participation in all other Regular Camp programs and facilities

 

The camp fee DOES NOT include transportation to/from Girona or health insurance in the event of injury.  Girona is easily reached by train from Barcelona/Barcelona airport. Participants are encouraged to have their own health insurance coverage.

 

 

PAYMENT

 

Please note that the number of participants is limited to help ensure a quality learning experience, and your spot is only guaranteed once full payment has been received.  Payment can be made via PayPal.com to sports_doc@yahoo.com

If you do not have the possibility to use PayPal please contact us for bank wiring information.

 

 

FINANCIAL SCHOLARSHIPS FOR THE 2016 CAMP

 

We recognize that not every player and his family can afford the full price to attend the 2016 Rick Mahorn Big Man Camp. While striving to cover our costs, we also want to make it possible for some players needing financial assistance to take part in this amazing experience.  Therefore, we have set up the following process. PLEASE READ CAREFULLY!

 

  1. Financial aid of up to 2/3 of the camp costs may be available, though most financial aid scholarships will be less than this amount.
  1. Financial aid will MOSTLY be given based on the amount you request (the less you ask for the greater your chances to get it). Requests will be considered in the order that they are received so the sooner you act the better your chances getting financial aid!
  1. Students and those NOT playing professionally (or whose player contract is less than 10.000 €) may be given greater consideration.
  1. To request financial aid, you should return the camp registration information and send a separate email to RickMahornBigManCamp@gmail.com with the following information:
  1. The reason for your request, stated in 1-2 sentences, together with as much information about your financial situation as you feel comfortable sharing.
  1. The amount you are asking for (in other words, the amount you wish to have subtracted from the full camp price)
  1. Within 3-5 days of receiving your request, we will reply with an amount we can give you – it may be the full amount of your request or it may be a portion of that amount.
  1. You will have 7 days to accept of decline the offer.  If you decide to accept your offer, make your payment of the registration amount agreed upon to the PayPal account – if you don’t have a PayPal account you can wire money to the camp bank account in the U.S.
  1. Finally, in the spirit of “paying it forward,” you will be asked to re-pay 2/3 of the financial aid you receive back to the Rick Mahorn Big Man Camp within 12 months of signing your first/next pro contract so that the money will be available for participants in future camps.

 

 

CANCELLATION POLICY

 

If for any reason the camp would be canceled – you will receive a FULL refund.

 

If you need to cancel before May 15, you will be charged a 75 € cancellation fee and will receive back the remainder of what you have paid.

 

Any cancellations on or after May 15 are subject to NO refund… BUT if we are in a position to refund some part of what you have paid we will make our most honest effort to do so.

 

FEEL FREE TO MAINTAIN ONGOING COMMUNICATION WITH US DURING THIS PROCESS. OUR GOAL IS TO HELP AS MANY PLAYERS AS WE CAN TO GET TO THE CAMP!
